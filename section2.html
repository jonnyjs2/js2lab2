Competitive Play

Valorant’s ranked mode tests teamwork and precision across various skill divisions. Players are placed in ranks from Iron to Radiant based on performance. The professional esports scene has rapidly expanded with international tournaments like Valorant Champions.

Maps and Strategy

Maps such as Ascent, Bind, and Haven feature unique layouts and mechanics like teleporters or open mid areas. Successful play depends on controlling choke points, predicting rotations, and coordinating utility use.
