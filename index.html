Valorant Wiki

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games. Released in 2020, the game emphasizes precise gunplay, team strategy, and unique character abilities. It blends elements of traditional FPS titles like Counter-Strike with hero-based abilities inspired by games such as Overwatch.

Meet the Agents

Jett

Duelist

Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.

Sage

Sentinel

Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Able to revive fallen friends and stave off aggressive pushes, she provides a calm center to a hellish fight.

Breach

Initiator

Breach fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. The damage and disruption he inflicts ensures no fight is ever fair.
