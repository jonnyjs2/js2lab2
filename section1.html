Gameplay Overview

Valorant features 5v5 matches where teams alternate between attacking and defending. Attackers plant a device called the Spike, while defenders attempt to stop or defuse it. Precision aiming, map knowledge, and communication are key.

Agents and Abilities

Each player selects an Agent, a character with unique abilities and an ultimate skill. Agents are categorized into roles such as Duelists, Initiators, Sentinels, and Controllers, allowing for strategic team compositions.
